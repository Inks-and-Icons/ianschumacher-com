02 — ABOUT

The full picture.

I'm a senior graphic designer and brand strategist with 12+ years of experience across the full design spectrum — from hands-on print production and screen printing, to running my own creative studio, to managing a multi-brand design system for one of the largest automotive franchise networks in the country.

At FullSpeed Automotive, I'm the sole creative responsible for 7 brands across 475+ franchise locations. That means I don't just make things look good — I build systems, manage brand integrity across dozens of stakeholders, and use modern tools (including AI-assisted workflows) to improve creative output at scale.

Before that, I founded and operated Printability LLC, a full-service creative studio in Bozeman, MT offering branded merchandise, screen printing, and graphic design. Running a business taught me things most designers never learn: how to own a project end-to-end, how to manage client relationships, and how to make decisions when there's no one above you to ask.

I'm based in Denver, CO and open to senior in-house and brand management roles — in Denver, Bozeman, Duluth, Spokane, or remote.
